# ROOTS
**Recurring Operators & Owners Table Society**

**Website:** [roots.la](https://roots.la) *(coming soon)*

---

## What ROOTS Is

ROOTS is a private council of builders, operators, and leaders in South Louisiana who believe success is stronger when built in trusted company.

It exists for men actively building businesses, careers, families, and legacy — men who carry responsibility and want peers who understand that weight.

ROOTS is not networking.
ROOTS is not a social club.
ROOTS is not a mastermind.

It is a recurring gathering of serious people committed to growth, accountability, and meaningful connection.

Membership is limited and invitation-based to preserve trust, honesty, and depth of conversation.

---

## The Culture

ROOTS operates on a few simple principles:

**Brotherhood Over Transaction**
Members show up as peers, not prospects. Business happens naturally, but ROOTS is never a sales environment.

**Builders First**
Every member is actively building something: a company, a career, investments, a family, a future. ROOTS attracts doers, not spectators.

**Honest Conversation**
ROOTS is one of the few rooms where successful men can speak openly about pressure, decisions, growth challenges, and leadership responsibility. Confidentiality is assumed. Trust is protected.

**Grounded Ambition**
Members value achievement without ego. ROOTS balances business success, personal discipline, family leadership, health, community, and legacy. The goal is not just winning — it is building something that lasts.

---

## The Members

ROOTS members typically include business owners, entrepreneurs, executives, operators, investors, and professionals leading teams or organizations.

They come from industries such as construction, technology, real estate, manufacturing, finance, healthcare, energy, and professional services.

Age and industry vary intentionally. What unites members is mindset: accountable, curious, driven, grounded, respected by peers, and committed to growth.

ROOTS is a room of equals.

---

## The Events

ROOTS gathers once each month in a private setting. Each gathering follows a consistent rhythm designed to create depth and continuity.

- **Arrival** — Members reconnect over drinks and conversation, transitioning from work into council.
- **Member Spotlight** — One member shares a current challenge, opportunity, or decision. The group provides perspective, experience, and counsel.
- **Council Discussion** — Open dialogue focused on real problems and real solutions. No presentations. No pitching. No posturing.
- **Intelligence Session** — A member or invited expert shares practical insight on leadership, capital strategy, technology, investing, performance, or growth systems.
- **The Round** — Each member shares a recent win, a current challenge, and a focus for the coming month.
- **Dinner & Conversation** — The evening closes with an unstructured meal where relationships deepen and ideas move freely.

---

## Membership

ROOTS membership is limited. New members join only through introduction or nomination by existing members. The strength of ROOTS depends on protecting the culture of the room.

---

## The Spirit of ROOTS

ROOTS exists for men who understand that success without community eventually feels empty. It is a place to think clearly, speak honestly, and grow alongside others committed to building lives of substance.

**Built deep. Built together.**
